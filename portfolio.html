Project 1 - Vegetarian Checker

I created this project to check and see if a purchased item at the grocery store is vegetarian or not using JavaScript. Just enter the UPC number under the barcode of a store bought item, which will then be processed through an API. The information gathered is organized into a table and will confirm if the item is considered vegetarian. Click on the image and enjoy!.
